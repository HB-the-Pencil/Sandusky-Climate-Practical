Extreme Weather Notes

Sandusky sits at the southern end of Lake Erie and is not at great risk for flooding, tornadoes, or earthquakes. firststreet.org rates Sandusky's flood risk as "minor". Looking at the graphs, July 18, 2016 had the highest wind speeds and precipitation out of the data collected.

There is some history of hail in Sandusky, with reports of quarter-sized hail in mid-July over the past 10 years. Based on our precipitation estimates, the 18th, 20th, and 22nd are the most likely days to have substantial precipitation, which could include hail.
